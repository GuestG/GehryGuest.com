About Me

As a UX Designer in Olympia, Washington I have a passion for creating intuitive and stunning UI's that draw user Engagement. My interest in UX was born during my time at University of Washington Computer Science program, when my peers wouldn't care about the look or use of the application as long as it functioned correctly. Since then I've been invested into front end design and usability. Always keeping up on the latest trends in order to create a pixel perfect designs that stand the test of both aesthetics and usability. In my free time I love to hike, listening to people's stories, and crafting worlds as a dungeon master for my D&D group.
